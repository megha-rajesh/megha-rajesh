ABOUT

Hi there! I'm a literature and journalism enthusiast, currently expertising in PR and business development for an edutech social enterprise.

I'm sure I've been told by a palm reader that I'd be successful in any career path I chose, and well, here I am! I've had the dream of working from home in my pyjamas all-day for a bunch of cool companies. It all started with my journey as a PR intern for a digital marketing agency. I prepared press releases, pitch letters, developed media kits, helped with backlinking, you know the drill. I then moved forward to working with an educational technology venture, where i take a lead on strategizing, planning, and executing digital marketing tactics.

Ever since I graduated with my astounding triple degree (in English, Journalism and Psychology), I've pretty much parked myself in front of my laptop every day. But when I do get a chance for a breath of fresh air, I still stay at home devouring instant noodles while binge-watching Netflix.
